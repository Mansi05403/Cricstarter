Power-Hitting

Power-Hitting is the most important part of batting. Today's generation wants to watch fast pace cricket and for fast pace cricket, we need power hitters. We need players like Chris gayle, Ab Devilliers, Andre Russel, etc. In this article, we will be teaching you some shear basics of Power-Hitting, if you are starting it from scratch as a batsman.

Power Hitting Tips

Power hitting skill is not just about smacking the ball as hard as you can; it is about mastering the physics in using the kinetic energy of the bat to exert the right force in reversing the direction of the incoming ball and lofting it over the boundary line.
